NEXIS

Student Technology Lab

NEXIS AI Entertainment Research Team
Spring 2025

Our project is all about helping people discover new music, especially from local or lesser-known artists. Platforms like Spotify are great, but they tend to push the same popular songs and mainstream artists, which makes it harder for users to find new or more niche music that really matches their unique taste. We wanted to change that by building a system that recommends artists based on your favorite genres and your location—so you can find local talent that you might not come across otherwise. Additionally, we used unsupervised learning using k means to suggest artists based on artists that were similar. We also designed an app concept where users can set their preferences and get personalized suggestions that go beyond what’s trending. In the future, we’d love to launch this on campuses like SU to help students explore local music scenes—and eventually expand it to other cities so more people can find music they truly connect with.
