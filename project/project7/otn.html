As Director and Executive Producer, I spearheaded all aspects of production for this award-winning music video series, which highlights diverse local talent through studio-based performances. Additionally, led multi-camera directing and technical directing while managing the post-production -- editing all episodes in Premiere Pro to deliver high-quality, on-schedule content
