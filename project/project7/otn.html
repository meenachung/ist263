Loud & Clear

A televised music performance and interview show at Syracuse University

Role: Executive Producer & Co-Director

As Director and Executive Producer, I spearheaded all aspects of production for this award-winning music video series, which highlights diverse local talent through studio-based performances. Through the coordination with musicians, crew, studio and audio engineers, I maintained smooth shoots and creative execution.
